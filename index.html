About

Seeing the world in pauses

I'm a photographer drawn to the spaces between moments — the light just before it shifts, the street just after the crowd passes. My work explores silence, structure, and the quiet drama of everyday life.

Based in Montreal. Available for commissions, editorial, and fine art prints worldwide.
