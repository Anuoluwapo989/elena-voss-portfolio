Frequently Asked

Before you reach out

What are your rates?

+

Rates depend on scope, location, and usage rights. Day rates for editorial and commercial work start at $3,500. Fine art prints are priced individually. Send me a note with your project details and I'll provide a custom estimate.

How far in advance should I book?

+

Ideally 4–8 weeks for commissioned work. Editorial assignments can sometimes be arranged on shorter notice. I'm currently booking for Summer 2026.

Do you travel for shoots?

+

Absolutely. I've worked in 34 countries and counting. Travel expenses are billed separately and discussed upfront. I'm happy to go wherever the project takes me.

Can I purchase prints directly?

+

Yes. Limited edition archival prints are available for most of my published work. Each print is signed, numbered, and shipped in museum-grade packaging. Reach out with the image you're interested in.

What's your turnaround time?

+

Editorial and commercial galleries are typically delivered within 2–3 weeks. Personal projects and fine art work take as long as they need — I don't rush the process.
